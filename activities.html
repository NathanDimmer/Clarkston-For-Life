Activities

READ

Reading is a vital source of learning and growing. We will provide reading opportunities each season of well-being, including a collection of books, articles or blogs.

#clarkstonforlifereads

WATCH

Some of us are visual learners! We will provide watching opportunities each season of wellbeing, whether it be movies, documentaries or videos.

#clarkstonforlifewatches

LISTEN

Some of us are auditory learners! We will provide listening opportunities each season of well-being, whether it be podcasts, music or books on tape.

#clarkstonforlifelistens

ATTEND

Some of us like to be out and about! We will provide opportunities for events each season of well-being, whether it be lectures, family fun or community gatherings.

#clarkstonforlifeattends

DO

Some of us need to be active! We will provide opportunities to get involved each season of well-being, whether it be active family ideas, workouts, or simple wellness strategies to try.

#clarkstonforlifedoes
